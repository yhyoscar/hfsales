Have Fresh, Keep Healthy, Be Joyful

Hephzibah Organic Farm is a family-owned certificated organic farm, located at Riverhead, eastern Long Island of New York. We grow organic mushrooms and vegetables, raise free range animals (chickens, ducks, goats, etc.), provide prepared food (ginger duck, organic beef mushroom dumplings, etc.), and host events (e.g. weddings, parties, family gathering, fellowship, art exhibition, light show, etc.).

Events

Besides of farming and producing organic food, we also host varieous events on the farm: wedding, birthday party, concert, light show, family gathering, corporate team building, church fellowship, camping, campfire, and so on. Our Summer Camping Program offer outdoor survival training for youths. In our three large studios, you will learn painting from our professional artists, make handicrafts, and potteries. Membership

Hephzibah Organic Farm is supported by our loyal members, in a community that we all believe in organic farming. We welcome everyone who shares the same value to join us! Members of Hephzibah Organic Farm enjoy the following benefits:

  • Members can always buy our organic products with the lowest price.
  • Members have high priority on limited or fresh products, e.g. fresh mushrooms and vegetables, cooked food.
  • We offer delivery or local pick-up service for members only.
  • Members can visit farm at anytime, and have free tour and photo booth.
  • Members have opportunities to join special events, e.g. fresh picking, parent-child activity, cooking campaign etc.
