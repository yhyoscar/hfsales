Have Fresh, Keep Healthy, Be Joyful

Hephzibah Organic Farm is a family-owned farm, located at Riverhead, eastern Long Island of New York. We grow organic mushrooms and vegetables, raise free range animals (chickens, ducks, goats), provide prepared food (e.g. ginger duck, organic beef mushroom dumplings, etc.), and host events (e.g. weddings, parties, family gathering, fellowship, art exhibition, light show, etc.).
