Membership

Thank you for being interested in our membership program! Members of Hephzibah Organic Farm enjoy many benefits, including:

  • Members can always buy our organic products with the lowest price.
  • Members have high priority on limited or fresh products, e.g. fresh mushrooms and vegetables, cooked food.
  • We offer delivery or local pick-up service for members only.
  • Members can visit farm at anytime, and have free tour and photo booth.
  • Members have opportunities to join special events, e.g. fresh picking, parent-child activity, cooking campaign etc.
  • (many more are coming...)

We currently have five available local pick-up sites: they are at the Farm, Stony Brook, Jericho, Flushing and Jackson Heights in Queens. Our pick-up locations are growing!

Our membership follows a prepaid-recharge model: the prepaid payment will be saved in your Hephzibah member saving account, and everytime when you buy products from us, the corresponding amount will be deduct from your saving account. You can recharge your saving account anytime you like. We'll remind you when your balance is low. There is NO registration fee or service fee.

We currently accept no-less-than $500 prepaid payment for new member registration, and we offer 10% discount for early-bird registration (before April 30, 2023). That means if you prepaid $500 to become a member, you will have a balance of $550 immidiately on your saving account.
